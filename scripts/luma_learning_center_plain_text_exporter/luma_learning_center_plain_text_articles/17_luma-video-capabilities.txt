Luma Video Capabilities =======================

Any video on your board can be extended, restyled, reframed, lip-synced, scored with music, captioned, analyzed, split apart, transitioned into, or used as a building block for larger productions.

Generate New Videos -------------------

Text-to-Video -------------

Describe a scene and get a video. Up to native 1080p resolution.

Text-to-Video with Audio ------------------------

Some models generate synchronized audio natively alongside the video (dialogue, ambient sound, music).

Image-to-Video --------------

Animate a Still ---------------

Turn any image into a video with motion, camera movement, and action.

Keyframe-to-Keyframe --------------------

Provide a start image and an end image, and generate the transition video between them. Great for controlled storytelling.

Modify & Transform Existing Videos ----------------------------------

Video-to-Video --------------

Change the style, look, or aesthetic of an existing video with adjustable strength control (subtle tweak to full transformation).

Extend Video ------------

Add more time to any video — extend it forward to continue the action, or backward to create a prequel moment. Adds ~5 seconds per extension.

Reframe & Resize ----------------

Aspect Ratio Changes --------------------

Convert a landscape video to portrait (or any ratio) using AI outpainting that intelligently fills in the missing areas. Perfect for adapting content across platforms.

Add Voice & Audio -----------------

Text-to-Speech Voiceover ------------------------

Generate natural voiceovers with emotion tags (excited, whispering, sad, etc.) and layer them onto your video.

Sound Effects -------------

Generate short SFX (5–22 seconds) to enhance your video.

Music -----

Generate full music tracks (with or without vocals) to score your video.

Lip Sync & Character Animation ------------------------------

Lip Sync --------

Take a video of a character and sync their mouth to any audio track with realistic movement.

Emotion & Expression Control ----------------------------

Advanced lip sync with control over the character's emotional reactions and expressions.

Captions & Subtitles --------------------

Transcription -------------

Extract word-level timestamps from any video's audio track.

Programmatic Captioning -----------------------

Render animated captions, subtitles, and text overlays directly onto video with full styling control.

Programmatic Video Editing --------------------------

Compositions ------------

Combine multiple videos, images, audio, and text layers into a single rendered output using code-based composition.

Transitions -----------

Add cinematic transitions between clips (fades, wipes, custom animations).

Overlays & Graphics -------------------

Layer text, shapes, and graphics on top of video.

Cinematic Transitions ---------------------

Transformation Injections -------------------------

Split a video at a specific point and insert a cinematic transformation effect (morphs, glitches, reveals) between the halves.

Analyze & Evaluate Videos -------------------------

Quality Assessment ------------------

AI evaluation of visual quality, pacing, and content.

Action Adherence ----------------

Structured validation: "Did X actually happen in this video?"

Brand Compliance ----------------

Check video against brand guidelines.

Consistency Checks ------------------

Verify characters, style, and settings stay consistent across multiple videos.

Visual Timing Analysis ----------------------

Extract motion peaks, impact moments, and scene transitions from video.

Audio Timing Analysis ---------------------

Extract beat detection, word timestamps, and silence points from video audio.

Multi-Shot & Storytelling Workflows -----------------------------------

Storyboards -----------

Build sequential multi-shot videos where each shot maintains visual continuity with the last.

Micro-Beat Production ---------------------

Produce consistent multi-shot videos using 5-second micro-beats with spatial references for things like music videos.

Image Sequence to Video -----------------------

Convert a series of images into a video by generating keyframe transitions between each pair.

Platform Adaptation -------------------

Cross-Platform Transformation -----------------------------

Adapt a video from one platform format to another (YouTube → TikTok → Instagram) using Ray2 video reframe or out-painting keyframes with nano banana, and then animating again with I2V per shot, or V2V with Ray3.14 Video Modify depending on video length.

Social Media Video Ads ----------------------

Turn videos into full ads with hooks, captions, and platform-specific optimization.

Full Production Workflows -------------------------

Product Review Videos ---------------------

Create authentic influencer-style product review videos with character and voiceover.

Product Video Loops -------------------

Create seamless looping product showcase videos.

Brand Campaigns ---------------

Use video as part of multi-asset campaigns with consistent visual identity.

Comic-to-Animation ------------------

Animate comic panels into sequential video.

Streaming Video Analysis ------------------------

Summarize & Transcribe ----------------------

Analyze streaming videos for content, structure, and transcription.

Import Videos -------------

Upload ------

Drop videos directly onto your board.

Web Download ------------

I can search the web, find video content, and pull it onto your board.
